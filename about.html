Origin Story

Kansas City is where it all began. Just a good ol' Mom and Pop shop. We decided to open up this restauarant to give people a nice place to code and enjoy some excellent food while they work. In a sense it is like a coffee shop but with Thai food. We have Free WiFi, so you can get all your work done while you are here.
